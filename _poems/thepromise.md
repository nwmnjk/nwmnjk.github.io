of shared language

beyond the page  
beyond the wall

the glassed unglaze  
behind the bounce  
under the gravity

anything but  
not just the  
bouncer

an image  
frozen capture  
formed shape  

inked ash traces  
stamped closed broken  
in the multiplicitous

adjacent  
transversal

*written in tension"
